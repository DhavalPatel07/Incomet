14 week journey will look like!

Learning is a gradual process and we achieve milestones and track progress week on week basis, here's what we will achieve by this course,

Week 1

At Incomet and Stock Market, mindset is very important. During the first week, we make sure that all of our students think in line with the right perspective and we all are on the same page so that the same goal can be achieved.

Week 2

Students achieve the basic understanding of the market, start possessing the SMART goal for their journey, and learn the roadmap to achieve.

Week 3

: Students are taught what not to do in the market, this is where basic knowledge is inculcated with smart trading processes, with a purpose to teach what not to do in the market and how not to make a loss.

Week 4

Learning of technical analysis and data driven strategies take place, this is a week of crucial learning and live trading with small capital.

Week 5

We move the students from a small swimming pool to the ocean, and achieve derivative knowledge. By this week students are ready to tap into the big boys club.

Week 6

Data driven strategies have been taught for trading in the market and practice of derivatives is done together in live markets.

Week 7

: Students are capable enough to mint 1-2k a day and are under the full time guidance of the mentor to correct the mistakes and focus most on their strong capabilities.

Week 8

Advance technical learning module, special system generation becomes the main objective and individual set up is being created for every student with the help of the mentor. By this time students are capable of minting 2-3k a day.

Week 9

Learning of investing journey, fundamental analysis, Multibagger look outs, and false alarm strategies are taught!

Week 10

Special learning module to uplift the trader and make them more confident in their money minting skills!

Week 11

Completion of special learning modules and as this week ends students are capable of generating 5-7k a day.

Week 12

Advance technical analysis strategies and Incomet option selling program is covered during this week.

Week 13

To reach maximum potential, specific strategies suited best for corporate employees, businessmen and students are taught to make maximum ROI with limited funds.

Week 14

By this week, we achieve psychology and sentiment learning, and achieve control over the mindset and emotions to create a bot type but flexible specialized system.
